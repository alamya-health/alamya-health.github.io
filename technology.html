Technology

One platform. A comprehensive genome.

Advanced sequencing technology

Long-read sequencing technologies are able to produce longer stretches of DNA sequence (known as a “read”) that enable detection of genomic variations commonly missed, or incompletely profiled, by conventional short read sequencing.

Using the Nanopore Promethion platform, this technology has excellent detection of genomic variation including single nucleotide variants, insertions and deletions (Indels), repeat expansions, structural variants, and copy number variants.

Major improvements in sequencing accuracy, coupled with the capacity to deliver a comprehensive genome, mean that long-read sequencing is primed for clinical use. Our approach involves validating this test and bringing it to the clinical market with CAP/CLIA accreditation.

Delivery at the point-of-care

Deploying alakai health solutions at the point-of-care enables rapid diagnosis when time matters most. Partnering with local clinicians and fostering expertise in harnessing comprehensive genomic, phenomic, epigenomic, and transcriptomic profiling to find answers for patients and families.

Integrating multi-omics

We have a phased approach of integrating our multi-omics data, first using these assays as functional readouts to aid in variant interpretation (e.g. to examine splicing, expression, or methylation differences). Then moving to advanced machine learning to read these biochemical profiles and predict disease states, informing diagnosis and enabling treatment discovery.

Capturing the phenome

The alakai health team has the clinical expertise and decades of experience in patient phenotyping, including the implementation and testing of standardized phenotype ontologies.
