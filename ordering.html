Online Portal

If this is your first time ordering from us, start by registering on our portal which can be accessed by pressing the “Order” button. This will allow you to access our ordering forms. Once registered, log in to the portal using your institution account and provider number. From there, you can choose the specific test you would like to order. Complete the ordering process. After submitting your order, you will receive an order confirmation email. This email will contain a summary of your order and serve as proof of your request. If you have ordered a kit(s) these will be sent to you as soon as the order is received. Now, you may send us the patient sample.
