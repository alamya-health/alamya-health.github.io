Online Portal

First time ordering? You will be prompted to register your institution or clinic at the start of the order form.

  1. Register (if required)
  2. Once registered, log into the portal using your institution or clinic account and provider number
  3. Choose the appropriate test
  4. Complete and submit the order form
  5. Expect a confirmation email - if you do not receive confirmation within 30 minutes (and have checked your spam folder), please contact
  6. If you ordered any test kits, these will be sent to you once the order is received
  7. Send us the patient sample
