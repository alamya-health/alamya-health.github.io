Genomic and precision medicine

alakai health offers a holistic approach to understanding rare diseases and delivering preventative, personalized, precision medicine.

Long-read sequencing technology

Long-read genome sequencing has the potential to improve molecular diagnosis by overcoming limitations of current tests, and reducing the burden of the diagnostic odyssey on patients and families.

Beyond the genome

Concurrent analysis of the epigenome and transcriptome directly couples functional biochemical evidence with comprehensive genomic variation, removing challenges in resolving variants of uncertain signficance.

State-of-the-art reporting

Contextualizing this complex multi-omic variation and providing insights into confirmatory testing, disease course, and potential interventions enables genomic medicine and medical decisions. Additionally interactive reporting of these data streams allows clinicians to choose how deep to dive into the patient's molecular profile.

Delivering solutions at the point-of-care

alakai health will deliver comprehensive diagnostic solutions embedded at the point-of-care, finding answers for patients and families within hours or days, instead of months to years.

alakai fulfills this mission by offering true whole genome interrogation with comprehensive interpretation and reporting.

This provides understanding of supporting evidence, next steps, and current interventions. alakai health strives to provide the information context that facilitates the right diagnosis enabling the right treatment for the right patient at the right time.
